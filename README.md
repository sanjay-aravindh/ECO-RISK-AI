# EcoRisk AI
### Ecosystem Intelligence Platform for Predictive MSME Credit Risk Management

## Overview

EcoRisk AI is an AI-powered credit risk management platform designed to help financial institutions predict MSME loan defaults up to **12 months in advance**.

Unlike traditional credit scoring systems that rely mainly on historical financial records, EcoRisk AI analyzes the complete business ecosystem by combining structured and unstructured data to detect early warning signals and provide explainable risk insights.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional MSME credit risk assessment faces several challenges:

- Low prediction accuracy
- Dependence on structured financial data
- Limited visibility into external business risks
- Reactive risk management
- Lack of explainable AI

---

## Solution

EcoRisk AI introduces an ecosystem-based approach to credit risk assessment by evaluating not only the borrower but also the surrounding business environment.

The platform combines:

- Financial Records
- GST Data
- Bank Transactions
- Business Documents
- Market News
- Industry Trends
- Customer & Supplier Dependencies

These inputs are processed using AI to generate a unified, explainable MSME Risk Score.
